Ashtanga Yoga

Ashtanga Yoga is a dynamic and physically demanding form of yoga that follows a specific sequence of postures, known as the Ashtanga Vinyasa Yoga Primary Series. Developed by Sri K. Pattabhi Jois, this method emphasizes the use of breath (prana) to synchronize movement and create a flow between the postures.
